SparkYa Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 29, 2026

1. Overview

SparkYa respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how SparkYa collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the SparkYa mobile application and related services.

Please read this policy carefully before using SparkYa. By using the app, you acknowledge that you have reviewed this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on the features you use, SparkYa may process the following information:

  • Account or profile information you choose to provide.
  • Content, preferences, or settings you create inside the app.
  • Device and diagnostic information, such as app version, device model, crash logs, and performance data.
  • Support communications you send to us.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve SparkYa.
  • To personalize app features and remember your preferences.
  • To troubleshoot bugs, improve performance, and respond to support requests.
  • To protect the safety, integrity, and security of our services.
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations.

4. Permissions

SparkYa may request device permissions only when needed for app functionality. You can manage permissions through your device settings. If you deny a permission, some features may not work as expected.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers that help us operate, analyze, or support SparkYa, and only as needed for those services. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, users, or services.

6. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as needed to provide SparkYa, resolve support requests, meet legal obligations, or protect legitimate interests.

7. Your Choices and Rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, or export of your personal information by contacting us. We may need to verify your request before taking action.

8. Children's Privacy

SparkYa is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

9. International Use

Your information may be processed in countries or regions where we or our service providers operate. We take reasonable steps to protect information according to this policy.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@sparkya.app.
